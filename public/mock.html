What changed vs. current

  • Typography: Inter everywhere. No more Archivo Black + Oswald + JetBrains Mono mix.
  • No uppercase / wide letter-spacing: Headings are sentence case. Reads like an office app, not a poster.
  • No diagonal stripes on cards. No grain texture. No corner crosshairs.
  • Pill-shaped chips (rounded, not boxy). Soft tinted backgrounds for status (blue/green/amber/red).
  • Cards: 1px slate-200 border + soft drop shadow on hover. 10px radius. No diagonal accent stripe at top.
  • Phone: bottom tab bar with 5 most-used tabs (Home / Orders / Review / Invoice / More). Same card style, tighter spacing.
  • Inputs: 36px height, 6px radius, blue focus ring. Search has an inline icon.
